Holiday Safety Tips

  • Be cautious with holiday lights. Always turn them off when you leave home, and check them for frayed wires.
  • Keep decorations safe and secure. Avoid placing sharp or breakable ornaments where they can fall and injure someone.
  • Check smoke detectors. Make sure your smoke and carbon monoxide detectors are in working order for extra holiday safety.
